About KSU CET Hostel Finder

Our Mission

KSU CET Hostel Finder was created by students, for students. Our mission is simple yet powerful: make it effortless to discover safe, affordable, and convenient accommodation near the College of Engineering, Trivandrum.

We understand the challenges students face when searching for suitable accommodation. That's why we've built a comprehensive platform that puts all the information you need at your fingertips.

What We Do

  • Curate up-to-date hostel listings with verified details and accurate information
  • Provide powerful search filters for distance, budget, amenities, and gender preferences
  • Ensure transparent pricing as provided directly by hostel owners
  • Offer detailed reviews and ratings from fellow students
  • Maintain contact information and location details for easy communication
  • Regular updates to ensure all information remains current and reliable

Our Community

We're part of the larger KSU CET community, working closely with the Student Assist Cell to provide comprehensive support to students. Our platform is just one way we're helping make student life easier and more connected.

Our Commitment

We're committed to maintaining this platform as a free, reliable resource for the student community. Our goal is to continuously improve and expand our services based on student feedback and needs.
